# **UX Architect: AI Strategy**

## **What We’re Looking For**

We are seeing a UX leader to help **shape the future of AI-driven user experience design at NetSuite**. We are looking for someone with design leadership experience who will operate as an individual contributor and consultant alongside the UX and Product teams. This person should be a visionary thinker with the knowledge and skills to create innovative AI-first solutions and drive meaningful change. They are not just an influencer, but a real leader deeply understands their craft and can apply that knowledge to execute to the benefit of the entire business.

Career Level - IC6

## **Responsibilities**

### **What You’ll Do**

- Contribute to shaping AI Strategy – Bring skills, experience, and knowledge to help lead NetSuite’s UX teams as they strive for AI-first design,
- Operate as a Leader – Support management and top-level leaders across UX and NetSuite Product in deeper AI understanding and application for UX. You will work with the highest levels of NetSuite leadership to share strategic thinking and guide decision-making.
- Inspire UX teams – Mentor, guide, and inspire UX team members to learn more and become more well-rounded. You will not be required to manage teams but should be ready and willing to share knowledge and provide thought leadership to all.
- Be able to work independently but always start with collaboration – This role requires independent thinking and skill but should never be done in a silo. We can only be successful as a company if we are sharing with and learning from all disciplines and levels.
- Specialize and Hone Your Craft – You may know a lot about UX overall, but you need to be an expert who is ready to specialize in AI-driven UX strategies more than any other part of design.

## **Qualifications/Skills**

- 15+ years experience in enterprise UX orgs, including time in leadership roles
- Proven impact from leadership in AI-driven environments
- System and “big picture” thinking with ability to hone that to individual, achievable actions
- Facilitation expertise
- Ability to collaborate with and influence cross-functional executives
- Masterful storytelling abilities
- Deeply understands customer problems
