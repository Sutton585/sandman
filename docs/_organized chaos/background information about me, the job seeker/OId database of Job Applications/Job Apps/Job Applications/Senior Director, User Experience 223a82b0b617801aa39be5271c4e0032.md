## **Job Description**

### About Us

EagleView is a leading provider of aerial imagery, property insights and software that transforms the way people work. EagleView holds more than 300 patents and owns a large geospatial data and imagery library encompassing 94 percent of the US population. EagleView provides the most accurate data, enabling customers in the government, construction, solar and insurance industries to make timely, informed and better decisions.

### Overview

EagleView, is a leader in geospatial technology providing solutions that revolutionize how its customers work. Known for its expansive 3 billion+ imagery library, EagleView property intelligence covers 94% of the U.S. population and offers imagery covering more than 65% of Canada’s four largest provinces. EagleView holds more than 300 patents and leverages its technology portfolio to offer cutting-edge software, imagery, and analytics across various industries, driving faster and smarter decisions.

We are seeking a visionary and strategic Senior Director of User Experience (UX) to lead and scale our global design organization. In this role, you will shape and champion our end-to-end product experience strategy, guiding a high-performing team of designers, researchers, and UX leaders. Your influence will extend beyond design execution—you will act as a critical thought partner to Product, Engineering, Marketing, and Executive Leadership to drive innovation and deliver experiences that differentiate our brand in the market. This is a unique opportunity to define the UX culture, processes, and strategic direction at a time of growth and transformation at the company!

### Responsibilities

- Executive Leadership & Strategy: Define and communicate a bold UX vision that aligns with company-wide business goals, serve as a key voice at the leadership table, influence strategic decisions with customer-centered insights, and foster a design-driven culture across the organization.
- Team Building & Leadership: Build, lead, and mentor a world-class UX team of designers, researchers, content strategists, and design ops professionals. Create career development paths, mentorship programs, and hiring strategies to scale the UX team.
- Product & Design Excellence: Establish and maintain design systems, research practices, and accessibility standards to ensure consistent, scalable experiences. Partner with Product, Engineering, and Customer Success to drive experience quality, usability, and measurable user outcomes.
- Customer & Market Insights: Champion user research and customer insights to shape the product roadmap. Evangelize the voice of the customer and advocate for their needs across the organization.
- Operational & Organizational Impact: Optimize UX workflows and operations to improve velocity and quality. Own budget planning, resourcing, tooling, and vendor management for the UX organization.
- Cross-Functional Alignment: Guide collaboration between UX, Product, Engineering, and Business stakeholders.

### Qualifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Design, Psychology, Computer Science, or a related field.
- 10+ years of professional experience in UX/Product Design, including 5+ years in a leadership or executive-level role.
- Proven track record of scaling design teams and delivering impactful user experiences across complex products.
- Strong expertise in design systems, accessibility, user research, and product strategy.
- Exceptional skills in design thinking, user research, prototyping, and information architecture.
- Excellent communication, storytelling, and executive influence skills.
- Experience driving change in fast-paced, agile, or high-growth environments.
- Experience mentoring other leaders and creating an inclusive, growth-oriented team culture.

### Preferred

- Demonstrated success aligning UX strategy with AI or data-driven product features.
- Experience in SaaS, enterprise, or geospatial technologies is a plus.
- Global design or research leadership experience.
