        ### Procurement Description
        
        The State University of New York wishes to award a single contract to one bidder that will provide a UX Designer to create user experiences for education administrative systems. The role involves designing user-centric solutions, understanding administrative staff needs, and working with the PMO to identify improvements. The UX Designer will collaborate with various departments, SUNY campuses, and external vendors to ensure project requirements are met.
        
        ### Contract Term, Work Location, and Other Work Information
        
        Contract term is two years with an option for a one-year extension.
        
        Primary work location is State University of New York System Administration, H. Carl McCall SUNY Building, 353 Broadway, Albany, NY 12246.
        
        Remote work within the United States may be allowed at SUNY’s discretion, with approved arrangements including specific connectivity requirements.
        
        Travel may be required to SUNY campuses or sites for stakeholder engagement, project meetings, or on-site assessments, depending on project needs. SUNY makes no reimbursement for travel or travel-related expenses, therefore, bidders should include all the costs of doing business in their proposed rates.
        
        ### Scopes of Service
        
        SUNY is seeking a talented and creative UX Designer to focus on developing intuitive and engaging user experiences for education administrative systems. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating user-centric designs and a deep understanding of the needs and challenges faced by administrative staff in educational institutions. This position will also engage with the Project Management Office (PMO), identifying critical areas for improvement and ensuring adherence to project management tools and processes. Additionally, this role will facilitate collaboration between departments, SUNY Campuses, and external vendors to ensure all project requirements are identified, procured, and available to meet the project schedule.
        
        ### Key responsibilities:
        
        - Collaborate with product managers, developers, and educational administrators to define and implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals, and user experience.
        - Execute all stages of the design process from concept to final hand-off to engineering, including wireframing, prototyping, and user interface design.
        - Conduct user research and testing to gather insights and validate design decisions.
        - Develop and maintain personas, user journey maps, and other user-centered design artifacts.
        - Create high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes to communicate design ideas effectively.
        - Ensure designs are consistent with our brand guidelines and standards.
        - Current with the latest UI/UX trends, techniques, and technologies.
        - Advocate for the end-user throughout the development process and ensure that user experience is prioritized.
        - Design solutions that streamline administrative processes and enhance efficiency.
        
        ### Experience:
        
        - 7 years of experience in managing higher education, government, and corporate
        
        projects with a broad breadth of experience in application development is preferred.
        
        - Experience providing strategic advice and guidance to senior management on
        
        business/functional-related matters.
        
        - Experience in evaluating and improving existing business processes and systems to
        
        enhance efficiency and effectiveness.
        
        - Experience in utilizing data analytics to drive business insights and identify
        
        opportunities for digital enhancement.
