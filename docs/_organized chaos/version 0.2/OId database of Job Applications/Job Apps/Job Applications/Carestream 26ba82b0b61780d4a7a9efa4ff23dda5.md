## **Position Summary:**

We are seeking a skilled Usability Engineer to lead the implementation and enhancement of the Carestream usability engineering process to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements. This individual will be responsible for ensuring seamless collaboration, alignment with regulatory requirements, and successful execution of usability engineering deliverables for medical device development. The ideal candidate will have strong interpersonal and organizational skills, experience in usability engineering, and a deep understanding of regulatory standards in a medical device context.

## **Position Responsibilities:**

- Ensure the Carestream usability engineering process complies with IEC 62366-1, ISO 14971, and FDA Human Factors Engineering guidelines.
- Review and update usability templates and process documentation to remain compliant with evolving standards.
- Develop and manage specific Usability Engineering Files (UEFs), ensuring complete and compliant documentation (e.g., usability plans, testing protocols, and reports).
- Conduct usability validation studies, as needed, to ensure device interactions minimize risk and enhance user experience.
- Support the internal team in addressing usability-related risks and integrating findings into development and risk management documentation.
- Provide usability insights throughout the design phase, aligning product decisions with usability objectives.
- Engage clinical teams to incorporate real-world user feedback into usability assessments and device improvements.

## **Required Skills & Education:**

- BS in Engineering, Human Factors, or a related field and 7+ years of experience in usability engineering for regulated industries.
- Excellent communication and organizational skills to manage cross-functional collaboration.
- Strong documentation skills, including experience reviewing and managing usability files.

## **Desired Skills:**

- MS in Human Factors, Engineering, or a related discipline.
- Strong understanding of IEC 62366-1, ISO 14971, and FDA Human Factors Engineering guidelines strongly preferred .
- 10+ years of experience in usability engineering, including working with external usability vendors.
- Proven experience working with external usability agencies or vendors.
