## **Job Description**

### About Us

EagleView is a leading provider of aerial imagery, property insights and software that transforms the way people work. EagleView holds more than 300 patents and owns a large geospatial data and imagery library encompassing 94 percent of the US population. EagleView provides the most accurate data, enabling customers in the government, construction, solar and insurance industries to make timely, informed and better decisions.

### Overview

We are looking for an enthusiastic Senior UX Designer with enterprise-level experience, including native mobile (iOS and Android, phone and tablet). You might be designing for navigating, measuring or annotating geo-based imagery or 3D models, job and project management, workflow tools, Machine Learning or Computer Vision, or numerous other interesting interaction models. A successful Senior UX Designer will exhibit a thorough understanding of user-centered design (UCD), be proficient in UX software, such as Figma, and be able to clearly and effectively communicate design processes, ideas, and solutions to teams and stakeholders.

### Responsibilities

- Design enterprise-level solutions with complex and nuanced interactions for web and mobile
- Own a business track, including multiple form factors and multiple solutions
- Conceptualize original ideas that bring simplicity and user friendliness to complex design roadblocks
- Partner with Product Managers, Engineers, and Researchers to define problems & goals, clarify personas, map user flows, prototype interactions, and design products
- Give and solicit feedback from cross-disciplinary partners
- Work with UX researchers and other stakeholders to conduct concept and usability testing and gather feedback
- Perform market and user research, including contextual inquiry, product feedback, data analysis, or competitive research
- Define the right interaction model and evaluate its success
- Develop wireframes and interactive prototypes around customer needs
- Work with UI designers and front-end developers to implement effective designs
- Present and defend designs and key milestone deliverables to peers and stakeholders
- Communicate design requirements developers
- Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices and standards
- Keep abreast of competitor products and industry trends

### Qualifications

- 7+ years of experience with end-to-end Enterprise-level product design
- Excellent communication skills
- Lo-fi & hi-fi prototyping skills
- Strong research, systems analysis (process and flow mapping), solution modeling (process and interaction modeling, as well as working with mental models), task flow analysis and design skills
- Proven UX design experience with a strong portfolio
- Have owned full process UX and design, in collaboration with product managers and engineers
- Have shipped products
- Have cross-platform design experience (web, android, iOS)
- Comfortable performing user research and interacting with consumers, even if your experience is not extensive
- Strong design skills with a clean, simple aesthetic
- Ability to solve problems analytically and creatively
- A self-starter who can work independently with minimal supervision

**Preferred Experience:**

- User research experience, including contextual interview, usability testing, remote feedback, surveys and feedback systems, and data analysis
- Experience creating and implementing a design system
- Product experience related to geo-based imagery navigation (panning, zooming, rotating, etc.), 3D model navigation in a touch environment, ESRI, Machine Learning/Computer Vision, and/or drone flight planning
- Experience working in an Agile/Scrum software development environment
- Experience with designing for accessibility (WCAG, Section 508 compliance, etc.)

This job description is not an exclusive or exhaustive list of all job functions that a workforce member in this position may be asked to perform. Duties and responsibilities can be changed, expanded, reduced, or delegated by management to meet the business needs of the company.
